## Categories of Work

### Engaging Networks Page Templates

ENgrid page templates are the foundation of every implementation. They support donation, advocacy, e-card, event, survey, premium, and other EN page types. Templates are mobile-first, accessible, and optimized for conversion. A majority of ENgrid clients maintain an active retainer with 4Site for ongoing enhancements.

See [Organizations Using ENgrid](/docs/v2/clients) for live examples.

### WordPress Promotions Plugin

The WordPress Promotions Plugin provides scheduling-aware on-site promotions including multistep donation lightboxes, pushdowns, overlays, floating tabs, and more. Promotions can be scheduled to the minute with support for Cloudflare cache busting.

See [WordPress Promotions Plugin Overview](/docs/v2/wordpress-promotions-plugin-overview) for full documentation.

### Email Templates

4Site designs and builds branded Engaging Networks email templates that align with your page template branding. Email templates use responsive design and are tested across major email clients.

### Analytics and Reporting

Every ENgrid implementation includes integration with your existing analytics tools. Common integrations include Google Tag Manager, GA4, Google Ads, Meta, Adobe Analytics, Matomo, and OneTrust. 4Site handles setup, custom reporting, testing, training, GDPR compliance, and ongoing support.

See [GTM Data Layer](/docs/v2/gtm-datalayer) for ENgrid's built-in analytics features.

### Take Action Landing Pages

Custom microservices that index Engaging Networks landing pages and display them on your website with full-featured imagery, photography attribution, and real-time fundraising or action-taker counts. These pages can include fundraising goal progress, action-taker goals, and category-based filtering with deep-linkable URLs.

## Custom Solution Highlights

The following are examples of custom solutions built on top of ENgrid's extensible architecture. These represent the types of enhancements that can be developed for your organization.

### Symbolic Gifts Shopping Cart

A highly customized giving experience that lets donors build a shopping cart of symbolic gifts. The solution supports multiple currencies and formatting, multilingual content, 15+ built-in color themes, per-card hover effects, inline upsell opportunities, and content-admin configuration through the EN page builder. Organizations that have tested this pattern have seen improvements in both conversion rates and average gift size.

### Post-Donation Giving (Regive)

A solution that presents a follow-up donation opportunity on the thank you page immediately after a primary gift is completed. The secondary gift can be designated to a restricted fund or serve as an additional contribution on top of the original gift. The form is auto-filled with the donor's information from the first transaction, minimizing friction.

See [Post-Donation Donation](/docs/v2/post-donation-embed) for the core ENgrid feature this builds upon.

### Cart Abandonment Recovery

A custom solution deeply integrated with Engaging Networks that applies configurable business logic to determine when an abandonment occurs and which existing supporters should be excluded from receiving the recovery email. The abandonment email is sent from Engaging Networks, ensuring it benefits from the platform's IP reputation and inbox placement.

### Multistep Donation Lightbox

A multistep donation lightbox with support for static images or video, monthly giving nudges, custom submit button animations, and branded frequency buttons. This pattern has been adopted across multiple organizations, with each customizing the experience to match their brand and audience.

### Interactive Quiz Experiences

An interactive quiz module that lets content admins build engaging user experiences using native Engaging Networks field types and content blocks. The solution breaks content into multiple steps, identifies right and wrong answers, and displays associated educational content and imagery after each answer. Organizations using this pattern have seen average on-page time exceeding 10 minutes.

### Social Proof and Wall of Signers

Widgets that display supporter activity — either a randomly sorted list of donors and their gifts (social proof) or an infinite scroll of advocacy signers (wall of signers). Both solutions are built with accessibility in mind and respect browser preferences for reduced motion.

### Custom Merge Tags

A merge tag generator that dynamically adjusts page text, imagery, and other content based on URL parameters. This allows a single EN page to serve multiple purposes — for example, showing different messaging depending on which email campaign, ad, or landing page linked to it.

### Premium Gift Displays

Flexible premium gift configurations with three distinct display themes that content admins control through the page builder. Each theme presents premiums differently to match the page context and campaign goals.

### Monthly Upsell Checkbox

An inline checkbox near the final submit button that lets one-time donors upgrade to monthly giving with a single click. The checkbox appears contextually and uses the supporter's selected amount to display the monthly conversion offer.

### Gated Content

A lightweight signup gateway for downloading content. A hidden "download URL" field is all that is needed for content admins to drop in a link to a PDF or other download asset. After form submission, the page automatically links to the download from the thank you page and includes it in the autoresponder email.
