4Site support retainers provide ongoing access to strategy, design, and development teams under a single, predictable engagement. Retainers are 4Site's preferred way to partner with nonprofits because they create continuity, reduce overhead, and allow for rapid response as priorities shift.

---

## Included Services

Support retainers cover both planned work and the day-to-day needs that come with running complex digital systems. This includes:

- **Dedicated Project Manager and Strategist** who oversee planning, coordination, and delivery
- **Monthly planning and check-in calls** to review progress, priorities, and upcoming work
- **Weekly written updates** summarizing work completed, in progress, and planned — including visibility into hours used
- **Regular recurring tasks** such as CMS updates, CRM maintenance, and design or performance reviews
- **Website and CRM uptime monitoring**
- **Real-time CRM error monitoring and reporting**
- **Monthly ENgrid core updates** — pulling in the latest upstream ENgrid Scripts changes, staging for your QA, and deploying approved updates
- **Bug fixes and issue resolution** for your ENgrid pages and integrations
- **Content editor support** for questions about the page builder, helper classes, and ENgrid features
- **Minor enhancements and feature requests** delivered within your retainer hours

## Retainer Benefits

Retainers are structured to give you flexibility while ensuring consistent access to a senior team that knows your system.

- **Priority scheduling** — Retainer clients receive first priority for scheduling work
- **Discounted hourly rate** — Retainers include the most discounted hourly rate available, and that same rate applies to any additional project work contracted while under a retainer
- **Free or reduced-cost access** to select 4Site-built Engaging Networks services and WordPress plugins
- **Flexible use of hours** within each quarter — unused hours can be banked and used at any point within the same calendar quarter
- **Floater hours** to absorb unexpected or time-sensitive needs without renegotiation
- **Team continuity** — Your dedicated team builds institutional knowledge of your setup, goals, and workflows over time

---

## Retainer Structure

To balance flexibility with accountability, retainers are structured with the following guardrails:

### Check-ins

One to two scheduled monthly check-ins to review progress, align on priorities, and adjust plans as needed.

### Flexible Hours

A set number of hours are reserved each month. Unused hours may be banked and used at any point within the same calendar quarter.

### Floater Hours

Ten percent of the annual hours are reserved as floater hours that can be used at any time during the year for unplanned or urgent needs — no renegotiation required.

### Urgent Work

Work flagged as urgent with immediate turnaround draws against retainer hours at a premium rate. This ensures fast response when you need it most while maintaining fair resource allocation.
